# Data Retention and Deletion Policy

Effective date: January 31, 2026

This policy describes how Rep Rummble retains and deletes data.
This is a template and must be reviewed by legal counsel.

## 1) Retention principles

We retain data only as long as needed for:
- Providing the service
- Security and fraud prevention
- Legal and regulatory requirements

## 2) Retention periods (default)

- Account data: retained while account is active
- Workout and meal logs: retained while account is active
- Support tickets: 24 months
- Security logs: 12 months
- Billing data (if applicable): per legal requirements

## 3) Deletion

- Users can request account deletion via support.
- We will delete or anonymize data within 30 days unless legal obligations require longer retention.

## 4) Backups

Backups are retained for disaster recovery and are deleted on a rolling basis.
Backup retention: 30-90 days (configurable by operations).

## 5) Legal holds

If we receive a valid legal request, we may retain data longer.
